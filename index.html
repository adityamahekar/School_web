About Our College

Pioneering technical education for over three decades

Our History

Established in 1985, we have been at the forefront of technical education, producing skilled professionals who contribute to industry and society.

Our Mission

To provide quality technical education that transforms students into competent professionals with strong ethical values.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in technical education, innovation, and research that meets global standards.

Admission Process

Begin your journey to a successful career

Eligibility

Students who have passed SSLC/10th standard or equivalent examination with minimum 35% marks in aggregate.

Academic Calendar

Admissions typically begin in June-July each year. The academic year is divided into two semesters.

Application Process

Applications are accepted online through the official website. Documents can be submitted in person or by post.

Academics

Comprehensive programs for diverse career paths

Computer Science Engineering

This program provides students with a strong foundation in computer science principles and programming skills. Students learn to develop software applications, work with databases, and understand computer networks.

Duration

3 years (6 semesters)

Intake

60 students per year

Careers

Software Developer, Web Designer, Network Administrator

Learn More

Electrical Engineering

This program focuses on electrical systems, power generation, transmission, and distribution. Students gain practical knowledge in electrical machines, power electronics, and control systems.

Duration

3 years (6 semesters)

Intake

60 students per year

Careers

Electrical Technician, Power Plant Operator, Maintenance Engineer

Learn More

Mechanical Engineering

This program provides comprehensive knowledge of machine design, thermal engineering, production technology, and automation. Students learn to design, analyze, and maintain mechanical systems.

Duration

3 years (6 semesters)

Intake

60 students per year

Careers

Mechanical Technician, CAD Designer, Production Supervisor

Learn More
