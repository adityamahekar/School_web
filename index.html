About Our School

Committed to excellence in education since 1985

Welcome to Karnataka Government School

Our school is dedicated to providing quality education to students from all backgrounds. Established in 1985, we have been at the forefront of educational innovation in Karnataka.

We believe in holistic development, focusing not only on academic excellence but also on character building, sports, and extracurricular activities.

Our mission is to create responsible citizens who will contribute positively to society. We follow the state curriculum while incorporating modern teaching methodologies.

With state-of-the-art facilities and a dedicated team of teachers, we strive to create a nurturing environment where every student can thrive.

Eligibility Criteria

Admissions are open to students who have completed previous grade in recognized school. Documents required include:

  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous Year Report Card
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Income Certificate (for scholarship)

Admission Process

Admissions typically begin in May-June each year. Follow these steps:

  1. Collect application form from school office or download online
  2. Submit filled form with required documents
  3. Attend interaction session (if required)
  4. Complete admission formalities and pay fees
