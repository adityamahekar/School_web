Admission Process

Begin your journey to a successful career

Eligibility

Students who have passed SSLC/10th standard or equivalent examination with minimum 45% marks in aggregate.

Academic Calendar

Admission typically begins in June–July each year. The academic year is divided into two semesters.

Application Process

Applications are accepted online through the official website. Documents can be submitted in person or by post.

Scholarships

Scholarships available for eligible students based on merit and economic background.

Academic Programs

Comprehensive programs for diverse career paths

Computer Science Engineering

This program provides students with a strong foundation in computer science principles and programming skills. Students learn to develop software applications, work with databases, and understand computer networks.

Duration: 3 years (6 semesters)

Intake: 60 students per year

Careers: Software Developer, Web Designer, Network Administrator

Learn More

Mechanical Engineering

This program focuses on designing, analyzing, and manufacturing mechanical systems. Students gain expertise in thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and machine design.

Duration: 3 years (6 semesters)

Intake: 60 students per year

Careers: Mechanical Technician, Design Engineer, Production Supervisor

Learn More

Civil Engineering

This program prepares students for planning, designing, and executing construction projects. Curriculum includes structural analysis, surveying, and construction management.

Duration: 3 years (6 semesters)

Intake: 60 students per year

Careers: Site Engineer, Structural Designer, Project Coordinator

Learn More

Electrical Engineering

This program focuses on electrical systems, power generation, transmission, and distribution. Students gain practical knowledge in electrical machines, power electronics, and control systems.

Duration: 3 years (6 semesters)

Intake: 60 students per year

Careers: Electrical Technician, Power Plant Operator, Maintenance Engineer

Learn More

Electronics & Communication Engineering

This program covers electronic devices, circuits, communication equipment, and systems. Students learn about analog and digital electronics, microprocessors, and wireless communication.

Duration: 3 years (6 semesters)

Intake: 60 students per year

Careers: Electronics Technician, Communication Engineer, Embedded Systems Designer

Learn More

Automobile Engineering

This program focuses on automobile design, manufacturing, and maintenance. Students learn about vehicle dynamics, engine technology, and automotive electrical systems.

Duration: 3 years (6 semesters)

Intake: 60 students per year

Careers: Automotive Technician, Service Advisor, Quality Inspector

Learn More

Commercial Practice

This program combines business knowledge with technical skills. Students learn about accounting, office management, business communication, and computer applications.

Duration: 3 years (6 semesters)

Intake: 60 students per year

Careers: Office Administrator, Account Assistant, Business Correspondent

Learn More
